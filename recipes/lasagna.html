Description(Fun Facts)

Lasagna Fun Facts: Lasagne is a wide, flat pasta shape and possibly one of the oldest. As with most other types of pasta, the word is a plural form. Lasagne meaning more than one piece of lasagna ribbon. The word also refers to a dish made with this type of pasta, in several layers interspersed with layers of ingredients and sauces.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 pounds Beef Chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablesoons fresh oregano
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tablesoon white or red vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper,onion,garlic and add the beef
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and tomato sauce
  5. Boil and drain lasagna noodles
  6. Heat the oven to 375°F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
